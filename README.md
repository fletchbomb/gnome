This is a digital version of a board game called Gnome Invasion created by Ross Flethcher.
It is an unfinished work in progress. Here are the latest rules:

# Gnome Invasion — Rules Reference

## 1. Game Summary
Gnome Invasion is a cooperative stealth and collection game where players control Gnomes exploring a house, collecting Socks, and avoiding Patrols.

---

## 2. Objective
Collect all required Socks and return them to the Laundry Room before the Trouble track reaches its limit.

---

## 3. Setup

- Place the Laundry Room tile at the center.
- Shuffle Room Tiles into a face-down stack.
- Shuffle Event Deck.
- Shuffle Trouble Deck.
- Place Patrols off-board.
- Each player:
  - Choose a Gnome
  - Place in Laundry Room
  - Start with empty Satchel

---

## 4. Round Structure

Each round consists of:

1. Event Phase
2. Player Turns (each Gnome)
3. Patrol Phase

---

## 5. Gnome Turn

Each Gnome has 3 Actions per turn.

### Available Actions

#### Move
- Move to an adjacent room through a door.

#### Explore
- Draw a Room Tile and place it in an open doorway.

#### Kick
- Push a target (Patrol, Gnome, or object) into an adjacent room.

#### Hide
- Avoid Patrol detection.

#### Interact
- Pick up or drop Items.

---

## 6. Movement and Exploration

- Rooms must connect door-to-door.
- Tiles can be rotated when placed.
- Dead ends are allowed but the house must remain expandable.

---

## 7. Items and Carrying

- Each Gnome has a Satchel.
- Default capacity: 2 items.
- Items include:
  - Socks
  - Goodies
  - Hats

---

## 8. Socks

- Socks are the primary objective.
- Dropped when Gnomes are caught.
- Can be picked up and carried.

---

## 9. Goodies

### Shroom
- Effect: Look at top 4 Room Tiles and reorder.

### Coffee Bean
- Effect: Gain +2 Actions this turn.

### Sticky
- Effect: Place in a room to affect Patrol movement.

---

## 10. Silly Hats

### Red Hat
- Effect: +1 free Move each turn.

### Blue Hat
- Effect: +1 Satchel capacity.

---

## 11. Trouble

- Certain effects increase Trouble.
- When Trouble increases:
  - Draw a Trouble Card
  - Resolve its effect

---

## 12. Trouble Cards

### Loose Satchel
- Effect: All Gnomes drop all carried items.

### Messy House
- Effect: Gnomes cannot Scurry.

### Hustle
- Effect: Lowest-ranked Patrol moves twice.

---

## 13. Patrol Phase

- Each Patrol activates in order.
- Patrols move according to their rules.
- If a Patrol enters a room with a Gnome:
  - The Gnome is caught.

---

## 14. Patrols

### Roomba
- Movement: Moves along right-hand wall.
- Catch Penalty:
  - +1 Trouble
  - Push Gnome 1 room
- Special:
  - Picks up 1 Sock per room entered
  - Kick to force it to drop Socks
  - Immune to Sticky
  - Removes Sticky from rooms it enters

### Dad
- Movement: Standard patrol movement
- Catch Penalty:
  - Gnome drops items
- Special:
  - Picks up Socks
  - Can be kicked to drop items

### Kid
- Movement: Standard patrol movement
- Catch Penalty:
  - Stuns Gnome
- Special:
  - Uses slingshot

---

## 15. Status Effects

### Stunned
- Gnome cannot act next turn.

### Sticky
- Patrol movement is restricted.

---

## 16. Events

### Vent
- Type: Positive
- Quantity: 2
- Effect: Add Vent to room.

### Boon
- Type: Positive
- Quantity: 2
- Effect: Add 1 Goodie.

### Laundry Chute
- Type: Positive
- Quantity: 1
- Effect: Add Chute to room.

---

## 17. Room Quirks

### Creaky
- Effect: Makes noise when entered.

### Dark
- Effect: Limits visibility.

---

## 18. Room Tile Reference

### Laundry Room
- Doors: N, E, S, W
- Quantity: 1
- Quirks:
  - Sock drop-off
  - Weaver present

### Hallway
- Doors: N, E, S, W
- Quantity: 2
- Quirks:
  - Creaky

---

## 19. House Progression

- As more rooms are placed:
  - Difficulty increases
  - Patrols become more active

---

## 20. Win Condition

- All required Socks collected and delivered.

---

## 21. Loss Condition

- Trouble reaches maximum.

---

## 22. Glossary

### Action
A unit of activity a Gnome can perform.

### Patrol
An NPC that moves and threatens Gnomes.

### Trouble
A global difficulty tracker.

### Satchel
Inventory carried by a Gnome.
